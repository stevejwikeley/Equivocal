The Gardens and grounds

500 acres of lush fields, gardens and couryard

slide-pics/gardens-01.jpg" class="ls-bg" alt="">
slide-pics/gardens-02.jpg" class="ls-bg" alt="">

The manor is set in surroundings so peaceful it almost like being in a dream.

Long warm days can be spent in the garden, the sun and in the fresh air, untroubled by the stresses and pains that come as we grow up. Manoir Equivocal’s grounds are evocative of that care free age, a scene from our memories of a blissful, summer evening from your childhood.

Whether you want to hold a party, wedding breakfast or something more bespoke, tell us about it over phone or email and we can work with you to accommodate any type of catering requirement from drinks and canape receptions to fork buffets or formal silver service dining.
