The entire Manoir Equivocal

The beautiful manoir surrounding buildings and grounds.

slide-pics/entire-manoir-01.jpg" class="ls-bg" alt="">
slide-pics/entire-manoir.jpg" class="ls-bg" alt="">

The entire Manoir and it's grounds can be rented, which includes the three bedrooms in the manoir, the dovecote, the cottage and all the gardens and courtyard.

A large structure often marks the site of somewhere special and Manoir Equivocal is no exception, in a far off place in France, there is a small manor that used to be the home of Nicolas Rolin: esteemed chancellor to the Duke of Burgandy whose troops captured Joan Va Eyck. He lived here together with his wife Guigone de Salins who founded the Hospice of Beaune, one of the worlds most charitable institutions.

The distinctive main house was built some one hundred and fifty years ago. There are two outer buildings housed in the grounds; one a dovecote and the other a farm cottage now a charming self catered guest house set apart in this quiet hide away, surrounded by green trees.

Manoir Equivocal is an ideal place to hide away, whether or not you are prone to poetry. The setting is poetic in itself. Framed by large trees. The elegant manor has a sense of absolute calm. No doubt if the walls of the classic building could speak, many anecdotes might be told of those who have stayed here.

Parting from the manor will indeed be a tale of sweet sorrow.
