About Le Manoir Equivocal

Le Manoir Equivocal is a  recently restored 500 year old manor nestled in the heart of Burgundy.

The manor is owned by Madame Mireille Lenoir-Senard, and run by her daughter Irene Lenoir. Both family members have a strong interest for China and have created a place where their strong ties and affections with the Far East could be conveyed.

Up until now the primary activity at the manor has been reaping the lush agricultural organic farmland, but in 2010, after ten years of restoration, the manor opened its doors as a bed and breakfast and members of the public were invited to hold events, parties and weddings at the manor and in its grounds. The farming will continue and develop into chinese medicinal plant farming in the future.

The manor also boasts an extensive wine cellar which the Senard family have built up in the area over many years. The manor can be rented for wine tasting and wines can be purchased from Irene on your visit.

In addition to this, an artist residency is planned to be put in place over the next two years, where Western and Chinese artists will be welcomed to stay at the manor, relax and draw inspiration from the historic heritage and serene environment. The manor will host Chinese cultural activities including tofu farming, Chinese cooking lessons and various Chinese customary events.

The manor will create an exciting environment where people from all over the world can meet and encounter one another's cultures, and imagination  in the playground that is Le Manoir Equivocal. The manor is a magical place hosting hundreds of exciting events throughout the year, so keep coming back to see what events we have planned each month.
