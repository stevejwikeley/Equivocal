Weddings at the manor

The team at Le Manoir Equivocal understand that your wedding is one of the most important days of your life and we want to make it as stress free as possible. Our wedding packages are designed to be flexible and can be adapted to suit your needs.

Manoir's reception rooms

This historical room has magnificent windows showcasing fantastic views over the land as well as a state-of-the-art bespoke lighting system to create the perfect atmosphere for post dinner dancing.

Gardens & Court Yard

Once you have completed your wedding vows, your photographer can whisk you away to secluded romantic areas within the Manor’s grounds, whilst your guests are served with champagne and canapés.
